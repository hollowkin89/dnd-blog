Dice!

While I do not have a massive dice collection, i still have more than the others in my dnd group, and everyone calls me a dice goblin. i have 91 dice, that being 1 set of 11 dice, 11 sets of 7 dice, and 2 individual dice, one d4, one d20, and a d10. apologies in advance for how garbage the images are! i had terrible lighting for all these photos rip

The dice sets

Transparent turquoise

this is one of the first sets i bought and i fricking love them. they kinda look edible i want to eat one.

Opaque White

these are just white with black numbers. there isnt much to say here.

Radioactive green and transparent purple

the green ones look like toxic waste. they look like hard candy even more than the turquoise ones. why.

i lost the purple d4 so i bought a single d4. it isnt the same colour, but idc. it adds character. i consider it a part of that set now lol.

Translucent grey and black swirl

they look like glittery, swirly, storm clouds. they also have glow in the dark specks, but my phone camera is trash so i couldnt get a decent photo to add.

marbled pink and black, glittery light blue

I have two of these sets because the place i got them from was doing a deal where if you bought two D&D items you would get some free lizardfolk minis and they were super cheap so why not. these look awesome. the first set i bought other than the set that came with the starter kit

multicoloured metal dice, marbled pink and blue, marbled dark blue and light blue.

I got the metal dice as a birthday gift from a friend of mine, and they're one of my favourite sets. because they have a bit of weight to them they are super satisfying to roll.

even though it is difficult to see in the image, the lighter blue is slightly shiny for both the pink + blue and the lightblue + darkblue dice.

red transparent

these are the very first set i got, it came with the starter set. this is a set of 11, unlike the other sets of 7. not my favourite, but i have a little bit of sentimental attachment to them.
